The time-traveling bottle

This is an empty plastic bottle.

How far back in time can this bottle travel before breaking down?

Year 2000

(25 years)

The millenium changed 25 years ago. Fun fact: people were worried that electronics with dates could not handle the change over to the year 2000!

The same year, Nintendo announced their newest game console; the Gamecube.

Year 1900

(125 years)

From 1903, the first massproduction of the automobile Model-T began.

Still not breaking down...

Year 1575

(450 years)

PET bottles takes at least 450 years to break down. It is long enough to witness Henry III be crowned king of France.

Lets take it a step further...

Year 1025

(1000 years)

In worse cases, plastic bottles can take up to 1000 years to break down. That is long enough to go back to the end of the viking age.

End

When a plastic bottle decompose, it will break down to microplastics and stay on the planet for way longer than a 1000 years.

Let's prevent plastic from today sticking around until 3025... Recycle the bottles
